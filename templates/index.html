Welcome to Zwavhudivhudi Academy

About Us

Zwavhudivhudi Academy (Pty) Ltd is a subsidiary of Zwavhudivhudi Trading, offering high-quality, practical skills training in electrical installations, technical trades, workforce development, and computer literacy.

We provide learners with hands-on training that equips them with industry-relevant expertise for long-term employability and career advancement.

Our Vision

To become a premier institution for vocational and skills development, empowering individuals with expertise in electrical installations, technical trades, workforce development, and computer skills.

Training Programs

Electrical Installation & Maintenance

Wiring, circuit testing, compliance with South African electrical standards, safety procedures, and fault finding.

Computer Skills & Programming

Python programming, HTML & CSS basics, computer literacy, Microsoft Office, and cybersecurity training.

Workforce Development

Hands-on training for tradespeople, unemployed youth, and business owners seeking industry-relevant skills.

Accreditation & Partnerships

Zwavhudivhudi Academy is in the process of obtaining accreditation with the Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO). We also collaborate with Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs) and local businesses to ensure industry recognition.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We offer free technical workshops for disadvantaged individuals, school partnerships for hands-on training, and promote eco-friendly energy solutions.
